ANNAPOORANI MOVIE


Annapoorani: The Goddess of Food is a 2023 Indian Tamil-language drama film directed by Nilesh Krishnaa in his directorial debut and jointly produced by Jatin Sethi and R. Ravindran under Zee Studios, Naad Sstudios and Trident Arts. The film stars Nayanthara and Jai in the title role, alongside Sathyaraj and Poornima Ravi. It follows Annapoorani, who aspires to become a chef, and the obstacles she comes across while trying to fulfill her dream.

The film was officially announced in July 2022, with the tentative title Nayanthara 75, as it is Nayanthara's 75th film in the lead role, while the title of the film was announced in October 2023. Principal photography commenced in April 2023 in Chennai along with a schedule held in Tiruchirappalli and wrapped by mid-September 2023. The film has music composed by Thaman S, cinematography handled by Sathyan Sooryan and editing by Praveen Anthony.

Annapoorani was released in theatres on 1 December 2023 and received mixed reviews from critics. After its streaming debut on Netflix, the film elicited accusations of promoting love jihad and hurting Hindu sentiments, resulting in it being taken down from Netflix worldwide.The music and background score is composed by Thaman S, in his fourth collaboration with Nayanthara after Anjaneyulu (2009), Greeku Veerudu (2013), and Godfather (2022); maiden with Krishnaa. The audio rights for the film were acquired by Saregama.[13] The first single "Ulagai Vella Pogiraal" was released on Nayanthara's 39th birthday (18 November 2023),[14] and the second single "Life Is On" was released on 23 November 2023.
